About TSP

TSP or The Saving Portal is an automatic cashback monitoring system that automatically scrapes the best cashback rates for a whole variety of stores for users to use and benefit from in order to survive and overcome these harsh times when there is still an ongoing pandemic negatively affecting many countries. There is also going to be a recession that will be worse than the 2008 recession and it will also negatively impact millions of people in terms of their financial standing, income, and much more.
However, to solve and help alleviate the financial issues of many citizens, The Saving Portal will allow users to save money and get money back from each purchase through our website by allowing users to view the best cashback rates of every store available in our site while also giving them an easy way to access and go inside these reward platforms and check out other benefits they offer for a particular store that the user has interest in buying a product from in which our web application displays it both numerically and visually in the form of a bar chart.
